The Reddit Fantasy Top Novels List is a bi-annual community-driven poll organized by the r/Fantasy subreddit. Every two years, users vote on their favorite fantasy books and series, creating a ranked list that showcases the most beloved works within the genre. These polls are highly anticipated in the community because they represent a snapshot of current tastes in fantasy literature, balancing long-standing fan favorites with newer, innovative works. The list often serves as a guide for readers seeking top recommendations from trusted fantasy fans.
